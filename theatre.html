On Stage Now & Coming

At the heart of The Mansa World is live performance — epic theatrical productions and orchestral concerts that bring Africa's greatest stories off the page and onto the stage. This is where history breathes, where legend becomes flesh, and where audiences are transported across centuries of untold glory.

Games, animation, and merch extend this world. But the stage is where it all begins.
