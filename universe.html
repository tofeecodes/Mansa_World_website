The Universe

A World Built on
Forgotten Legends

Before the maps were redrawn, before the gold was seized and the names erased — there was a world of kings, scholars, warriors, and explorers whose stories shook the earth beneath their feet.

The Mansa World is the living, breathing universe of Africa's greatest untold heroes — brought to life first on stage, then through games, animation, and myth reborn.

Every character, every production, every world we build is rooted in real history — and charged with the power of what that history means today.
