Introduction

Africa's Story.
Told by Africa.

✦ ✦ ✦

The Mansa World is a bold new cultural and creative platform dedicated to telling Africa's greatest stories at a global scale. Founded by Ghanaian playwright, performance poet, and producer Chief Moomen, the project reimagines the legacy of Africa's past through world-class live performance, music, storytelling, and immersive experiences.

Produced by Bambu Heritage Productions and promoted by Masala Corporate Limited, The Mansa World is formally endorsed by UNESCO through a partnership agreement signed at its Paris headquarters in February 2025.

At its core, The Mansa World is not just a production — it is a growing cultural universe, designed to transform African history and heritage into globally relevant, commercially viable intellectual property.

The Vision

"Africa must own, produce, and distribute its own stories at a global scale."
— The Mansa World Manifesto

The Mansa World is built on a simple but powerful idea: African narratives are not niche cultural exports — they are mainstream global content capable of reaching audiences across every continent.

The platform begins with the rich historical legacy of West Africa, particularly the story of Mansa Musa — one of the most iconic figures in world history. From this foundation, The Mansa World is designed to expand across all regions of Africa, building a unified storytelling universe that reflects the full depth and diversity of the continent.

UNESCO Partnership

The Mansa World was officially launched at the Paris headquarters of UNESCO in October 2023. This culminated in a formal partnership agreement signed in February 2025 under the framework of the General History of Africa initiative — aimed at advancing the global visibility and accessibility of African history through creative platforms.

🏦

Afreximbank Engagement

The project has built a significant relationship with the African Export-Import Bank, featuring prominently at the Bank's major platforms — Annual Meetings, the Intra-African Trade Fair (IATF), and CANEX events. Through these engagements, The Mansa World has been positioned at the highest levels of African finance, trade, and business.

✈️

Global Reach

Since its inception, The Mansa World has grown from a local creative concept into an internationally recognised cultural platform — with engagements spanning Africa, the Caribbean, the Middle East, Europe, and the United States, across 33 live performances and counting.

🌍

The Mansa World Caravan — London 2026

London will host the first Mansa World Caravan in 2026 — a travelling cultural festival integrating live theatre, orchestra performances, exhibitions, screenings, pop-ups, and immersive storytelling experiences. A major milestone marking the platform's arrival on the global stage.
